This is where you can read all the hottest reviews of STL style 'za.

While St. Louis style pizza is reviled the country over, they can be enjoyed when the appropriate hot sauce is deployed. But the savvy pizza consumer knows that some area pizzas are unable to be saved at any spice level.


These are the best pizza spots and why:

  1. Cecil Whittaker's
    • Literally the cheapest and ALWAYS two-for-one large and extra-large pies.
    • Tobasco can easily hide provel flavor
  2. Imo's
    • Ridiculously expensive for 12 ounces of pizza
    • Takes Siracha to taste anything but cardboard
  3. Elicia's
    • Somehow still feels like a bad deal even though it's cheaper than Imos's
    • Not good. No hot sauce can save it. Even ghost peppers lose all flavor when added.
